My name is Matthew, though my friends call me Matti. I was born and raised in Kansas City, Missouri. Shortly after high school, I moved to Seattle, where I earned my degree at the University of Washington and spent eight years.

Later, I moved to Europe, where I worked at a number of different companies and eventually settled in the Netherlands. Throughout it all, while holding down full-time jobs, I kept writing. At a certain point, I decided to take myself seriously as a writer.

I write novels in the early mornings, late at night, and on weekends, mostly propulsive psychological thrillers set in Europe.

I've owned this domain since the early 2000s. Over the years, platforms arrived—WordPress, Squarespace, Wix—and social media narrowed personal expression into short-form videos. Somewhere along the way, I felt like I lost my own creativity on the web.

This personal website is a way back. A way to reconnect with the web as a creative space. To experiment again. To find my footing without worrying too much about polish or purpose. I built this site myself.

This page is mine. It's simply a place for the things I want to share: writing, thoughts, fragments, experiments. A place to be.

You're very welcome to join me here if you're interested. If you're not, that's completely fine too. No hard feelings.

Thanks for being here.
